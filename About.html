About me.

​I started college as a mechanical engineer. I switched into computer science when my friend got me to audit one of the intro classes. That is when I began to have a passion for computer science. After seeing all the possibilities that I was able to do one of the branches that got me interested was web development. I actually started my journey by learning web development through https://www.w3schools.com/html/ a free online website that gives you lessons. Through classes and spending time coding for fun I also began to get an interest into UI development. What I enjoyed about UI development was the ability of coding and seeing the code take shape and form!


By practicing each day I didn't want to put the skills I learned to waste. The biggest lesson that I learned is that in the field of computer engineering you have to put in your own time to get ahead of the game!
